- Vault mode: `isolated`
- Render mode: `obsidian`

## Architecture
- Raw sources remain the evidence layer.
- Wiki pages are the human-readable synthesis layer.
- `.openclaw-wiki/cache/agent-digest.json` is the agent-facing compiled digest.
